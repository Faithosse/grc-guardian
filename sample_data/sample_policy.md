# Sample Data Protection Policy
## Small Charity Example

This policy outlines how we handle personal data.

### 1. Data Collection
We collect names, email addresses, and phone numbers from donors and volunteers.

### 2. Data Use
Data is used for communication about our activities and fundraising campaigns.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do not share data with third parties without consent.

### 4. Security
Passwords are required for all systems. We use cloud storage.

### 5. Retention
Data is kept for as long as the relationship continues.
